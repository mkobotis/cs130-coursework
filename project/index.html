About Notewordy

Created for my CS 130 final project, Notewordy is a thesaurus and writing resource for anyone who writes, whether it be for school or for fun. Improve your writing and expand your vocabulary! Find synonyms, antonyms, and definitions. Discover new words. Have fun!

Created by Maryarita Kobotis.
